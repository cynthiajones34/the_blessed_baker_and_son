The Story Behind the Name Our Story

Born from a son's belief in his mother's gift

It started the way the best businesses often do, not with a business plan, but with a child's pure enthusiasm. Ina's son loved her cupcakes so much he became her first delivery guy, selling them to his classmates at school. The kids loved the cupcakes so much they ate the paper liners too.

That story became the name. The Blessed Baker and Son. It is a family business rooted in faith, joy, and that signature cream cheese frosting that nobody else seems to make quite right.

"Customers consistently tell me they feel blessed by the cupcakes. I pray over the ingredients and my work. It is my calling."
